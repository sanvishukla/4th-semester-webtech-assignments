Ingredients

4 slices White/Brown Bread

2 slices of Cheese Slices

4 slices Bacon

2 Tomatoes

1/2 cup Butter

Instructions

Step 1

Place cheese on two bread slices.
Put bacon on the other two slices.
Add sliced tomatoes on top of the cheese.

Step 2

Create sandwich halves by joining the bacon and cheese sides.
Spread butter on the outer sides.

Step 3

Grill until golden brown and cheese is melted

Step 4

Slice diagonally.
Enjoy your quick Bacon and Cheese Grilled Sandwich!
